How it works

From position to post
in seconds

01
Connect your account
Link your Kalshi API key or Polymarket wallet address in Settings. Takes less than a minute.
02
Take a screenshot
Screenshot your position from the Kalshi or Polymarket app and upload it to Predicted.
03
Paste the market URL
Copy the link to the market from Kalshi or Polymarket and paste it into your post.
04
Post it
Your position is verified and your post appears in the Markets feed. Your reputation is on the line.
